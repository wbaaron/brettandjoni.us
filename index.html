The First Meeting

It started at the aptly-named Glory Bound restaurant in Tuscaloosa, AL.
Or rather, it would have, if Joni hadn't been feeling ill earlier that day. Instead, our story started a few miles down the road. See, Brett had a good friend named Nick. Nick had a girlfriend named Lauren. And Lauren? Well she had an old childhood friend named Joni. Joni worked at a university in Birmingham, but had been invited to Tuscaloosa for dinner and drinks with her friend Lauren. Dinner was missed, but feeling better, Joni made the 50 mile trip to Tuscaloosa. She met her friends out on the town, and that's when Brett first saw her. After a night of getting to know each other, Brett decided he needed to stay in touch and made the decision to ask for her phone number. A few knee-shaking and butterfly-in-stomach seconds later, he had the prize. The next day (he couldn't wait), a message was sent. A date was set. An adventure began.
